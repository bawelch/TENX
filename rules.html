How the game works, what each mode does, and how scoring is calculated.

Goal
Fill the ten ranks in the correct order for the current question within 5 submits.
Green
Correct answer in the correct position. It locks and cannot be changed.
Yellow
The answer is somewhere in the top 10 but is not in the correct position.
Red
The answer is not in the top 10 for the current question and is removed from future choice lists.
Prioritised answers
Yellow answers are prioritised at the top of all active choice lists until they are resolved or turned green.
Modes
Classic+: top-10 hint only, no scoring.
Modern: Classic+ plus scoring.
Modern+: Modern plus higher/lower directional hints for yellow answers.
Debug: Modern+ plus manual answer-pool selection, question selection, random-question button, and the detailed discovery breakdown.
Scoring
Final board score: green = +120, yellow = +45, red = -15.
Discovery score: first yellow and first green turns add bonus points based on how early they were found.
Notes
In non-debug modes, answer-pool and question controls remain visible but are disabled.
