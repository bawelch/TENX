Rules

Click a rank to choose from the answer pool. Green means correct answer in the correct position and it locks. Yellow means the answer belongs somewhere in the top 10, but not there. Red means it is not in the current top 10 and will be removed from future choices.

Green
Correct answer, correct rank, locked in place.
Yellow
Correct answer set, wrong rank, prioritised in the picker.
Red
Not in the current top 10, removed from future choices.
Attempts
You get 5 submits per question.
Scoring A
Final board score depends on green, yellow and red results on your last visible board state.
Scoring B
Discovery score depends on which turn a correct answer first became yellow and which turn it first became green.
Data model
Answer pools live centrally in one file; question definitions are generated separately and can be extended cleanly.
